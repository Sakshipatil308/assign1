Travelling

Traveling is the preferred spelling in the U.S. Travelling is the
preferred spelling in the UK or in the Commonwealth.
This American-British spelling difference carries for other forms:
traveled or travelled and traveler or traveller.



Aside Contet

Travel is the movement of people between distant geographical locations.
Travel can be done by foot, bicycle, automobile, train, boat, bus, airplane,
ship or other means, with or without luggage, and can be one way or round trip
